Here is a timeline of Isaac Asimov's life:

  • 1919 - Born in Petrovichi, Klimovichi Uyezd, Russian SFSR.
  • 1928 - Became a naturalized American citizen.
  • 1941 - Astounding magazine published the 32nd story Asimov wrote, "Nightfall".
  • 1942 - Published the first of his Foundation stories. Married Gertrude Blugerman.
  • 1956 - Published "The Last Question", where the computer Multivac famously responds "Insufficient data for meaningful answer...".
  • 1964 - His social science fiction novelette "Nightfall" was voted by the Science Fiction Writers of America "the best short science fiction story of all time".
  • 1992 - Died at the age of 72 in Brooklyn, New York City.
